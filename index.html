Makhana of India

Makhana (Fox Nuts), also known as lotus seeds, are a treasured superfood of India, traditionally harvested from lotus ponds. Naturally light, crunchy, and rich in nutrients, makhana is valued for its high protein, low fat, and easy digestibility. At LeanMakhana, we ensure careful sourcing and hygienic processing to preserve purity, taste, and nutrition.
