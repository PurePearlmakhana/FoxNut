Makhana of India

Indian Makhana (Fox Nuts) are renowned for their purity, light crunch, and exceptional nutritional value. Grown naturally in lotus ponds, makhana is a superfood trusted for generations.

At LeanMakhana, we deliver premium-quality makhana, carefully harvested and processed to meet international standards. Our mission is to bring healthy snacking to the world.
